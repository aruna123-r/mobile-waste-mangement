FAQs

1. Why should I recycle my old mobile?

To prevent e-waste pollution and recover valuable materials.

2. Can I donate my old working phone?

Yes, donating to refurbishing organizations is encouraged.

3. How do I locate e-waste recycling centers?

Use manufacturer take-back programs or certified local recyclers.

4. Is it safe to throw batteries in trash?

No, batteries contain toxic chemicals and must be recycled properly.

5. Should I remove SIM cards before recycling?

Yes, always remove SIM and memory cards before recycling.
